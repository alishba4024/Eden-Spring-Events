Eden Springs Events—a full-service event planning and design production firm—has become one of the world’s most celebrated and sought-after event companies.


As event architects we create one of a kind experiences for discerning private and corporate clients. Founded in 2002 and based in New York City, Eden Springs Events has received national acclaim from top industry publications including vogue, Harper's Bazaar, The Cut, and Brides, among others.


EVENT ARCHITECTS


Our team thrives on creating extraordinary, one-of-a-kind weddings and events, and seeing them through to spectacular conclusions. A Fête event is raved about time and again, because every facet of the experience speaks the same language. We are one of the few companies that conceptualizes, designs, plans and oversees all key elements in-house. By not outsourcing, we are able to control quality and ensure a cohesive vision. Our main services are planning, event design and paper product design, however we customize our service offerings to match clients’ needs.

TAILOR-MADE STORYTELLING


Part of our brand DNA is the ability to discover our client's needs. We deep dive into their personalities and wishes. Through this relationship, we discover parts that are unique to them, but we are also able to expose deeper layers that they didn’t know they wanted. The heart and soul of our team excels at an ultra-high level of workmanship and detail. We take pride in conceptualizing, planning and designing all key elements of our events in-house. This allows for unmatched quality control and ensures that the vision stays cohesive.
